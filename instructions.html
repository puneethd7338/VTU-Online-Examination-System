VTU Semester Examinations Protocols

Welcome to the VTU semester examinations. To ensure fair and smooth conduct, please adhere to the following protocols:

  • On-Time Arrival: Arrive at the examination center at least 30 minutes before the scheduled start time.
  • Valid ID: Bring a valid University -issued ID (College ID).
  • Hall Ticket: Bring a valid Hall Ticket which is issued from the University.
  • No Cheating: Any form of cheating, including using unauthorized materials or electronic devices, is strictly prohibited.
  • Follow Instructions: Listen carefully to the invigilators and follow their instructions throughout the examination.
  • No Communication: Do not communicate with other candidates once inside the examination hall.
  • No Early Leaving: Candidates are not allowed to leave the examination hall in the first hour or the last 15 minutes of the exam.
  • No Disturbance: Maintain silence and avoid any disturbance to fellow candidates.
  • Required Materials: Bring necessary writing instruments and other materials as specified for each exam.

Failure to comply with these protocols may result in disciplinary action, including disqualification from the examination.
